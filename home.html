Simply put, a kolache is an eastern-european version of a savory doughnut. It is a buttery yeast bread stuffed with everything from gourmet scrambled eggs to fresh smoked sausage. Originally topped with fruit, the humble origins of the kolache received a southern style kick in the pallet resulting in a Texas breakfast staple.

More importantly, a kolache is a tradition; a heritage of flavor. It is the moment in the morning you wish you could make last forever. From the caramelized nutty notes in the outrageously delicious bread to the perfectly planned combinations in the fillings, it will be exactly what you crave as you start the day.
